The problem for beginner student runners

Many students want to be more active, but existing fitness apps are built for experienced athletes, not beginners trying to move more in their own neighbourhoods.

Students often struggle because:

  • Most apps feel competitive, data‑heavy, and intimidating.
  • There is little guidance for building sustainable, low‑pressure habits.
  • Progress is hard to see, so motivation drops quickly.
  • Running is rarely linked to the idea of improving local communities.

How FirstStrides supports sustainable student running
